About

Project Green is made by a team of people who are just as concerned as you are about the environment. Through this platform, we wish to motivate and inspire the society to aim towards a sustainable future. Today, we face various problems in the form of environmental degradation, but with the help of the information we provide, we want the people to know that there is still hope, and that together we can achieve what couldn’t be achieved in the past….

Some Statistical Data

As children — though it depends which decade you grew up in — we often heard more about “pollution,” than we did “carbon emissions” or “greenhouse gases.” But essentially, yes, carbon emissions are a form of pollution. Carbon emissions affect the planet significantly, as they are the greenhouse gas with the highest levels of emissions in the atmosphere. This, of course, causes global warming and ultimately, climate change.
